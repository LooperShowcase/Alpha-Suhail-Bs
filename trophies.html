Real Madrid is the only club to have won three consecutive titles in the European Cup/Champions League twice, first in 1955–56, 1956–57, and 1957–58, and second in 2015–16, 2016–17, and 2017–18. In May 2022, they won a record-extending fourteenth European Cup, making it five titles in the last nine seasons.
